Project Lead The Way at BHS

Belton High School offers a wide variety of Project Lead The Way classes. These classes focus on biomedical science, computer science, and engineering. These courses are; Principles of Biomedical Sciences, Human Body Systems, Medical Intervention, Biomedical Innovation, Computer Science and Software Engineering, Computer Science Applications, Computational Problem Solving, Cybersecurity, Introduction to Engineering Design, Principles of Engineering, Digital Electronics, Computer Integrated Manufacturing, Manufacturing Technician Certificate Program, and finally Engineering Design and Development. All these programs helps kids find good future careers and engage and challenge them in new ways. Course descriptions below.
